Services

An Integrative Wellness Space specializing in integrative mobility & mindful movement practices.

Manual Sessions One-on-One Small Group Full Group Private Group

Manual Sessions

Therapeutic hands-on work targeting joint mobility, soft tissue restrictions, and postural patterns. Each manual session is individually assessed and applied — addressing the root of tension rather than its surface expression.

Sessions integrate joint mobilization, myofascial release, and breath-centered regulation to create lasting structural change rather than temporary relief.

Book a Session

One-On-One Practices

Private movement and practice sessions designed around your specific body, history, and goals. No group pace, no generic sequence — just a focused, intelligent approach built around where you are right now.

Whether your goal is increased mobility, injury recovery, nervous system balance, or expanded movement awareness, your practitioner designs each session to serve your precise needs.

Book a Session

Small Group Classes

Guided mobility and movement sessions in an intimate group of 3–6. Close enough for individual attention, connected enough to build a practice community.

Small group classes offer structure, consistency, and a supported environment where your progress is seen and guided — not lost in a crowd.

View Schedule

Full Size Group Class

Open community classes bringing together people of all experience levels. A welcoming space to explore guided mobility, breathwork, and mindful movement — accessible, consistent, and grounding.

A great entry point for new students and a reliable practice anchor for experienced movers. All levels are welcome and supported.

View Schedule

Private Group Practices

Curated sessions for 2–8 people who want to share a structured wellness experience — friends, couples, family members, or small teams.

All the depth and personalization of private work, combined with the energy and connection that comes from moving and growing together. Designed around the group's shared goals and individual needs.
