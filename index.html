No experience is required. Our practitioners meet you exactly where you are and build from there. We work with complete beginners and experienced movers alike.
Myofascial Release is a hands-on technique that gently releases restrictions in the connective tissue (fascia) surrounding muscles and joints. It restores range of motion, reduces chronic tension, and supports the body's natural structural balance.
